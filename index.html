About Our Firm

Newport Beach Personal Injury Attorneys

Dedicated Personal Injury Advocates Serving All of California

At Manning Law, APC, our Newport Beach personal injury attorneys represent clients injured in vehicle accidents, slip-and-fall accidents, dog bites, and other serious accidents. We are your local and national litigation firm, aggressively defending the rights of our clients and always fighting for you.

Accidents may cause serious injuries that can affect every aspect of your life — physically, emotionally, and financially. After suffering a serious injury, you may face medical bills and struggle to earn a living to cover the costs of your care. We can help you pursue the damages you are owed.

Our team stays current with California’s personal injury laws so we can provide guidance that aligns with the realities of our region. High-traffic areas like the Pacific Coast Highway and Crystal Cove State Park are no strangers to accidents. With deep regional knowledge and legal experience, we’re equipped to advocate for you every step of the way.
